ROYAL ENFIELD BULLET 350

Specifications

One of the oldest and most popular bikes in India is the Bullet 350 from Royal Enfield. It was first manufactured in 1932. Since then, the vehicle has undergone innumerable alterations. In India, this model is the entry-level offering from Royal Enfield. The 185 kg hefty bike sports a 5-speed manual gearbox. The single-cylinder, air-cooled, fuel-injected engine is capable of delivering similar torque and power performance as the Classic 350.
